to Egypt

Egypt is a country in northeastern Africa and part of Asia. It is famous for its ancient history and landmarks like the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the temples in Luxor. The Nile River flows through Egypt, providing fertile land for farming in the midst of the Sahara Desert. Egypt has a long history of being a cultural and economic center, and today it remains important in regional politics and culture.
